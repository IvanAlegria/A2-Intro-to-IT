Team Profile Results Summary/Discussion

The results gathered from the 16 personalities or Myers Briggs test show the individual a description of who they are and why they do things the way they do; thus some compatibility factors could be derived from the different personalities. For example, Anin and Ron who were labelled as ENFJ type or “protagonist” work well with Kelvin who is INFP or a “mediator” as Anin, Ron and Kelvin are equally intuitive, so can efficiently cater to each other’s demands in a group. Conversely, kelvin’s personality type doesn’t work well with Albert's as they have contrasting ways of seeing the world this could be because their minds inherently prioritise information differently which leads to very distinct viewpoints on what's important. Altaf and Ivan on the other hand have personality types that are great at offering well-balanced ideas as well as solutions to problems considering if they can work well together. Nevertheless, the personality types that are compatible or conflicting with each other based on the Myers Briggs test are just a reference and not indicative of the group's actual synergy.
