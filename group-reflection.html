Group Reflection


Group Activity


What went well?

Every meeting that we had was organized in a very sensible way. We took turns to organize meetings rather than one person taking all the load. Every meeting had agendas and we talked about how far we have worked on our parts. We used websites to see our availability & organize a meeting time. The due date of the assessment made it possible to relax and target for perfection rather than make it quick and just skim through work. It was made possible by one of the teammates to convince an IT (Information Technology) personnel to do the interview the interview was on time, and we faced minimal problem recording the interview (There was some background noise & the zoom disconnected suddenly) But nonetheless it was overall a great interview from my perspective. The questions were categorized. The answers were insightful. The good thing about the group is the communication. Some people might find it hard to talk to new people, but we did not find it to be a problem. Everyone took part to ask questions in the interview & in general meetings all of us talk about how they are doing with their part of the work. Everyone was in cooperation of helping each other & was polite enough to ask if anyone needed any help with their part. Everyone was continually active in teams as well uploading files, or any content related to assignment. All above everyone was very honest about the work. If someone had problem doing their part on time, they were honest about it.


What could be improved:

One of the problems were finding time for everyone to attend meeting. One of the team members was on work break while attending meetings & so that was problematic for everyone to join the meeting on time. Fortunately, every meeting was recorded so anyone who might fail to join in could later watch what was discussed throughout the meeting. Other than that, everyone was doing their part but not submitting on the collaborative document or not uploading beforehand the meeting. Teammate needed to be told to upload their work. Lastly teammates were very invested in their own work rather than working as a team. This points a double-edged sword. Even though all of us was trying to perfect our own parts it left us not to check anyone else’s work. Leaving every part as a solo work.


Surprising Elements:

The interview was very quick to set up and we did not do any retakes to make any improvements. It was very surprising since it was the first time, we did it. We learned a lot from each other about individual concept and insight.


Things we learn about the group:

Everyone in the group had weakness and strength.
